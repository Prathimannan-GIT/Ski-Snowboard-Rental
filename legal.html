Equipment Rental Agreement

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Equipment Ownership: All equipment remains the sole property of Apex Alpine Rentals. The customer assumes all responsibility for the equipment while in their possession.

2. Rental Period: The rental period begins at the scheduled delivery/pickup time and ends upon the successful return of the equipment to an Apex Alpine hub or agent.

3. Late Returns: A late fee equivalent to a full day's rental will be charged for equipment not returned by 6:00 PM on the scheduled return date.

4. Damages: The customer is liable for the full retail cost of the equipment in the event of loss, theft, or serious breakage, unless a Damage Waiver was purchased.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our rental services.

  • We collect name, contact information, and physical measurements (for gear fitting).
  • Payment data is processed securely through encrypted third-party providers.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
